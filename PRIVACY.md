# RedLight Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

RedLight is designed to work privately and offline.

## Data use

- RedLight does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.
- RedLight does not contain analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs.
- RedLight does not connect to the internet.
- If you enable sunset-to-sunrise scheduling, macOS provides an approximate location after you grant permission. RedLight rounds and stores that coordinate locally in its sandbox so it can calculate sun times offline.
- You can deny or revoke location access and use custom schedule times instead.

## Screen access

The default Compatibility Overlay does not capture or inspect screen contents.

If you explicitly enable the optional High Clarity renderer, macOS requests Screen Recording permission. High Clarity processes live display frames locally with ScreenCaptureKit and Metal to create a red-only image. Frames are never saved, analyzed for content, uploaded, or shared. Disabling High Clarity stops every capture stream immediately.

## Stored preferences

Filter, schedule, shortcut, and onboarding preferences are stored in the app’s local macOS container. Removing the app’s container deletes them.
